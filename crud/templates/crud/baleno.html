The Baleno is the most important car to Maruti Suzuki. It has been a runaway success and for a car that costs well in excess of Rs 10 lakh, to sell almost 10,000 units every month over the last six years is just incredible. With ever growing competition, M.S has finally come out with a brand new Creta that promises to be more premium and a new class benchmark. Prices have gone up too but so has the feature list.In terms of size, the new Baleno is longer and wider but it is 30mm shorter than before. Like the recently launched Maruti swift, the new Baleno’s design will receive mixed reactions from the crowd. In pursuit of trying to make the baleno as attractive as possible, Nexus’s designers have gone a bit overboard, especially with the nose and tail. Up front, you get a massive hexagonal grille that is outlined by a chrome strip that looks a tad too shiny. Then you have the ice cube three element LED headlamps that look stylish with the LED DRLs sitting on top of them. The turn indicators are placed lower down in the fog lamp
